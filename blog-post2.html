Just wrapped up an incredible 5-day Innovation, Design, and Entrepreneurship Bootcamp in hashtag#coimbatore , and I'm buzzing with excitement! This immersive experience challenged me to think outside the box, develop creative solutions, and gain valuable entrepreneurial insights. The warm reception and thoughtful gifts at Sri Ramakrishna Engineering College created the perfect atmosphere for an exhilarating adventure.

Key Takeaways:

Day 1 commenced with a captivating address from Prof. (Dr.) Sitharam T.G, the chairman of All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and Mr. Abhay Jere, the vice chairman of All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) igniting the excitement for the journey ahead. It was a day brimming with the prototype showcase stall and insightful sessions led by Dr. Muthipurni and Mr. Vishal Nair, exploring the realm of 'Design Thinking for Entrepreneurs.

Day 2 kicked off with Mr. Vishal Nair's engaging talk on key entrepreneurial principles. We learned about customer-centric problem-solving and empathy in business. In the afternoon, a honeybee farmer entrepreneur shared valuable insights on small-scale business, inspiring our entrepreneurial aspirations.

Day 3 began with Mr. Anand Raj explaining the 'Value Proposition Canvas.' We then had enlightening sessions by Mr.Vishal Nair, Mr. S. Manikraj, and Ashfin Francis covering various topics. In the afternoon, we had an exciting tour of SREC's Electric Vehicle Lab and SPARK Incubation Foundation, enhancing our learning journey.

Day 4 started with future entrepreneurs sharing Bootcamp impacts. We had sessions on 'Digital Presence' by Mr. Franklin Davidson and 'Starting Fundings' by Mr.Vishal Nair. Mr.Naveen Krishnamurthy discussed inclusivity and diversity. In the afternoon, we attended the 'Jubilant Tamil Nadu Global Expo & Knowledge Summit 2024'.
